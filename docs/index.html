Welcome!

The research group PLG started on 2003 from the Scalab research group at the Department of Computer Science, Universidad Carlos III de Madrid, Spain. We are a specialized group on automated planning, decision-making and machine learning technologies. You can find us in the Sabatini building at the lab 2.1.B16.

In PLG we develop algorithms for acting in complex environments using automated planning and machine learning. We offer a highly qualified human team and our research topics include:

  • Artificial Intelligence
  • Automated Planning
  • Heuristic Optimizations
  • Machine Learning
  • Reinforcement Learning
